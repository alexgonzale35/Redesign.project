Partners

Increasing Support

Partnerships allow us to leverage the resources, expertise, and networks of other organizations to maximize our impact on social causes.

Building Trust

Collaborating with reputable organizations can enhance our credibility and build trust among donors, volunteers, and the communities we serve.

Einstein Bros

Einstein Bros. Bagels has a large customer base and is deeply embedded in local communities. This partnership facilitates engagement with a diverse audience and promote participation in donation initiatives.

Plato's Closet

Partnering with Plato's Closet aligns with sustainability and environmental conservation goals by encouraging clothing donations and promoting reuse and recycling of clothing items, It contributes promoting a more circular economy.

Canes

Raising Cane's has a strong brand presence with a loyal customer base. This partnership allows us to tap into our extensive reach and visibility, increasing awareness for our cause.
