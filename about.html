ABOUT

Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands, Inc. (2C3H), exits to serve the unhoused, unsheltered and the less fortunate. We believe ministry and service begins in the heart. Our mantra is “Feel a Need… Fill a Need. We simply exist to share the message of Jesus Christ one full belly at a time. We stand on the scripture in Matthew 9:36…When he saw the crowds, he had compassion on them, because they were harassed and helpless…

-Our Mission is to be moved with compassion and offer the hands and help needed to alleviate the suffering of the hurting, the overlooked, the forgotten, and the lost.

What We Do

We seek to reach out to those who suffer spiritually, emotionally, and physically. Our domestic cities of service include Dallas, Fort Worth, Grand Prairie, and surrounding areas. Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands typically serves 200-400 meals weekly. We take out essentials: food, water, hygiene, coats, blankets, tents, sleeping bags. We are so blessed to work alongside great groups, partners, and individual donors and supporters that make sure we have needed resources.

What We Value

We have a God-given action item—to open our hands and give to those in need. We try to be sensitive to their needs and serve them with dignity. We understand may not be able to fully eradicate homelessness, but we can do our best to try. It takes a lot of support from partners to achieve our mission. A huge part of our success is aligning ourselves with those who share our passion and commitment. So, I say to the one reading this, you are an important part of making our mission sustainable and successful.

Meet The Founder

Hi, my name is Patricia Rigsby-Scott and I'm a licensed and ordained minister and wife to my wonderful supportive husband, Cedric. He's my preparer, picker upper, and packer. He helps and supports me by letting me do what I do, and I appreciate him for that! I have always had the desire to help. Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands was birthed in 2009.
