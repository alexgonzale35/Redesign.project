ABOUT

Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands, Inc. (2C3H), exits to serve the unhoused, unsheltered and the less fortunate. We believe ministry and service begins in the heart. Our mantra is “Feel a Need… Fill a Need. We simply exist to share the message of Jesus Christ one full belly at a time. We stand on the scripture in Matthew 9:36…When he saw the crowds, he had compassion on them, because they were harassed and helpless…

-Our Mission is to be moved with compassion and offer the hands and help needed to alleviate the suffering of the hurting, the overlooked, the forgotten, and the lost.

What We Do

2C3H is all about being Active in Missions—locally and abroad. We seek to reach out to those who suffer spiritually, emotionally, and physically. We currently have ongoing feeding programs in Kenya Africa, and have adopted a special needs home there to support with food. We have donated two wheelchairs to help with mobility-impaired children. Our domestic cities of service include Dallas, Fort Worth, Grand Prairie, and surrounding areas, and Texarkana.Where we are today. Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands typically serves 200-400 meals weekly. We travel to various homeless encampments, under bridges, and on the streets. We take out essentials: food, water, hygiene, coats, blankets, tents, sleeping bags. We are so blessed to work alongside great non-profits, groups, and individual donors and supporters that make sure we have needed resources.

Meet The Founder

Hi, my name is Patricia Rigsby-Scott and I'm a licensed and ordained minister and wife to my wonderful supportive husband, Cedric. He's my preparer, picker upper, and packer. He helps and supports me by letting me do what I do, and I appreciate him for that! I have always had the desire to help. Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands was birthed in 2009.
